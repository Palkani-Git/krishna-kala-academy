Founder’s Message

Smt. Kavitha Raghunathan

“"Every movement in Bharatanatyam is a prayer. Every note in a Bhajan is an offering to the Divine. At Sri Krishna Educational Academy & Visakha's Fine Arts, we do not merely teach art — we light the lamp of culture within each student's heart. When a child learns to express the stories of Lord Krishna through dance or music, they forge an unbreakable bond with our heritage. That bond is my life's purpose and this academy's highest calling."
