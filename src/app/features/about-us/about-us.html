Founder’s Message

Dr. Gayathri Saravanan
Founder & Director

"Sri Krishna Educational Academy & Visakha's Fine Arts was founded with a vision to preserve India's rich cultural heritage while inspiring the next generation through education and the performing arts. We are committed to nurturing every student's talent with discipline, dedication and creativity. Beyond classroom learning, we encourage our students to shine through cultural events, stage performances, television programs and national competitions, enabling them to grow with confidence and excellence. Every success achieved by our students is a reflection of our shared passion for art, culture and lifelong learning. We invite every aspiring learner to join our academy and become a part of this beautiful journey of tradition, excellence and inspiration."
